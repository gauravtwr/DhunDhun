Our Story

Gifting, the kawaii way

DhunDhun started as a small Instagram shop and grew into a full gifting brand — built on cute design and genuine care for every order.

Where we started

From Instagram DMs to a home for kawaii gifting

DhunDhun grew out of our Instagram page, where we shared Korean and Japanese style bags, stationery and cute finds one post at a time. As orders grew beyond DMs, we built this website to make it easier to browse, filter by who you're gifting, and order in a couple of taps — while keeping the same WhatsApp-first, personal ordering experience our customers already loved.

Today, DhunDhun sources and curates gifts across categories — from playful kids' toys to elegant corporate hampers — all with the same pastel, kawaii-inspired eye.
